Online Use


Aarhus University has an Internet connection can be used by all staff and students. When using the Internet exposes you also your machine - and your data - at risk. Therefore, you must always be vigilant. Even if your machine has a fully updated antivirus software , operating system, etc. You risk that a website you visit is infected with malicious code that our systems do not yet know. Remember that even "respectable" sites being infected - typically through the banner ads that fund many websites. So you should exercise common sense when you are on the Internet; be careful what you click!
